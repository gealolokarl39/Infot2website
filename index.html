Welcome to Libertad, Antique

The Banigan Festival celebrates the rich weaving tradition of Libertadnons. We tell the stories of the weavers, the crafting process, and cultural heritage.

History of Banig

The banig is a traditional Filipino woven mat, and in Libertad it has deep cultural roots. Generations of artisans have passed down the craft, binding community identity with artistry.

Weaving Process

The process starts with sourcing leaves like pandan or bariw, drying and dyeing them, cutting into strips, and then weaving them in patterns. Each design represents creativity and heritage.

About Us

We are committed to preserving the banig weaving tradition, supporting artisans, and enriching cultural awareness through the festival and related programs.

Services & Programs

We host weaving workshops, cultural tours, artisan training, and festival exhibits to empower the community and celebrate heritage.
